Hey! I'm Ling, a first-year Master's student in Pure Mathematics at Nankai University, under the supervision of Xiao Yao, with an expected graduation in June 2027.

My current research interest lies in complex dynamics, with additional interests in related areas including parameter space, ergodic theory, exponential dynamics, fractal dimension and non-autonomous dynamical systems.

I received my undergraduate degree in Finance from Shanghai University of Finance and Economics, where I focused on Quantitative Finance. I also spent a semester at UC Berkeley as a visiting student, which laid the groundwork for my interdisciplinary interests.

My life philosophy is to gain a deeper understanding of the world. You can find my recent work on Blog and explore the About section to find some fun stuff. Feel free to get in touch if you are interested.
